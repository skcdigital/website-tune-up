Pricing FAQs

Do I pay everything upfront?

50% deposit to start, 50% before we go live. EFT only. You always own the domain and the site.

What if I need something not listed?

Send me a WhatsApp with what you have in mind — I'll quote it within 24 hours, fixed price.

Are care plans compulsory?

No. Your site will keep working without one. A care plan just means I keep it healthy, updated, and answer your edits.

Do you do payment plans?

Yes — for Business and Pro packages I can split into 3 monthly payments. Just ask.

What if I'm not happy?

You see a working draft before you pay the second half. If we can't get it right, you pay only the deposit and walk away with the work done so far.

Not sure which package fits?

Tell me about your business in 1 WhatsApp message and I'll recommend the smallest package that does the job.
