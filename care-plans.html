Common Questions

Do I need a care plan?

If you can't afford for your site to be down or out of date for a week, yes. If you built it once and don't care, no.

No contracts. Cancel anytime by sending me a WhatsApp — your site stays yours.

What if my site wasn't built by you?

That's fine. As long as it's a standard WordPress, static, or React site, I can take over the care.
